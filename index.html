5 Tips For Creating a Successful PR Packet For An Event

Assembly Time: 45 minutes
Effectiveness Rating: 100% effective

Public Relations packets are powerful tools for getting your bussiness kickstarted. These packets contain information given to interested members of the media by an organization that wants more attention.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Pen
  • Paper
  • Laptop
  • Music
  • Snacks
  • Patience

  1. Introduce Your Organization.

    An explnation of what your organization does, why your organization does what it does, and who it does it for. It is also important to include a contact person and their contact information.

  2. Tell Them About Your Product or Service.

    Since the product or service is what generates income, it is important to highlight the good and minimize the bad. If you're offering a new product, then explain why is it significant

  3. Write A FAQ.

    Writing a section that talks about questions that are frequently asked keeps you from wasting time answering the same questions over and over.

  4. Write Short Biographies Of Important Members.

    You'll want to skip the boring stuff, like where they went to highschool or their college degree. Instead, write about unique aspects of the members like their reason for wanting to join the organization.

  5. Create Multimedia Specifics To Your Organization.

    It is becoming more common to include slideshows or photos. When customizing your multimedia for the event, you're essentially boosting the effectiveness of the strategy. Think of it as a long letter to reporters explaining why and how you want your product to be covered.
